The Problem
Your published PDFs need to change — a font license expires, a merger requires new branding, a backlist title needs updating for a new platform — but the source files are gone, outdated, or the rework would cost days per book. Tools like Acrobat or Callas try, but they break the layout. So the titles sit untouched.
How FontSwap Works
1. Upload your PDF. We detect every font in the document automatically.
2. Pick replacements. Choose from Google Fonts or upload your own. Map one font or many.
3. See the result. Preview your first 3 pages instantly. No reflow: line breaks, page breaks, and spacing stay fixed.
Built For
Font relicensing — Switch from expensive embedding licenses to open-source alternatives like Source Sans or Noto, without retypesetting.
Post-merger rebranding — Roll out a new corporate typeface across hundreds of existing titles.
Backlist revival — Prepare older titles for new platforms and markets without recreating them from scratch.
Quality corrections — Fix broken characters, wrong fonts, or encoding issues in published PDFs.
